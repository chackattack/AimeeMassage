About Aimee
True Passion for the Art

From teachers to strangers to family members, Aimee massaged everyone she came across. She carried around all sorts of new massage toys and studied the newest and traditional pressure points and massage trends. At 50 years old with a full time job as a project manager for State Comp and taking care of elder-in-laws, Aimee decided to add on another challenge: massage certification. Aimee completed her massage license by World Massage on December 2015. Massage by Aimee is a product of true love and passion. She plans on massaging only part time so you better book now!

Bright and Outgoing Personality
